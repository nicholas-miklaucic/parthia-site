Basic Combat Calculator

This page helps you calculate survival probabilities and outcomes for combat in Fire Emblem games.

  • The reason the specific game matters is because different games have different ways of silently modifying hit rates.
  • Even in the full table, outcomes with a less than 0.1% chance of happening are excluded.
  • Genealogy of the Holy War and Thracia 776 calculate critical hits differently from other FE games, so this calculator doesn't accurately report critical hit damage for those games. (It would require knowing the attack and defense of the units, which is overkill for a simple combat calculator.)
  • "Brave", in this context, means hitting twice outside of the normal combat order. This is the effect of the namesake brave weapons in many games, the Amiti, and many others.
